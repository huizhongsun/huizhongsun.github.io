Rong xi zhai tu - Ni Zan

An Illusion or A Truth

- Blandness

The limitless clarity of the sky answers the emptiness of the water, which spreads to fill the entire central portion of the scroll. A thatched roof, supported simply by for poles, presents the only sign of a possible human presence. But no one is sheltering beneath its eaves. Nothing here strives to incite or seduce; nothing aims to fix the gaze or compel the attention.
