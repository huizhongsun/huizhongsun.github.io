The Planck Universe - Ryoji Ikeda

Inspired by his exchange with mathematicians and physicians, the artist turned to the space of particle physics and physical cosmology—as measured in Planck units—for his audiovisual installation “micro | macro”. Negotiating unfathomable scales in his work, Ikeda puts both the concept of infinitesimally small particles that elude human perception [micro] against the observable universe [macro].
